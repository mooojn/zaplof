1. Social Media Presence
- Instagram, LinkedIn, Facebook Page, Behance, Dribbble.
- Post project showcases, before/after mockups, 15-sec reels of builds, and testimonials.
- Use branded templates (logo, blue/black tone, same font).

2. Platforms to Find Them:
- Facebook Groups (e.g. “Entrepreneurs in UK”, “Startups in Pakistan”, “Small Business Owners”)
- LinkedIn Search (filter by “Founder”, “CEO”, “Marketing Manager”)
- Fiverr / Upwork (use to find leads even if you don’t sell there)
- Reddit & Discord (Startup/Marketing channels)
- X (Twitter)
